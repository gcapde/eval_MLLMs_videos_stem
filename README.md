# MLLM-Based Assessment of STEM Projects Videos in K-8 PBL Contexts
Repository for the article "MLLM-Based Assessment of STEM Projects Videos in K-8 PBL Contexts", submitted to AIET Conference 2027, 7th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education Technology.

The repository includes the prompts used to analyze videos from 2024 and 2025, corresponding to the Minecraft category of a national robotics and programming olympiad. The files included in the repository are the following:
* General definition of the Minecraft category of the 2024 edition, which is used as part of the prompt to analyze videos ([prompt_dataset.txt](https://github.com/gcapde/eval_llms_edutech_assessment/blob/main/prompt_dataset.txt)).
